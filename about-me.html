About me

I like to create things. Especially creating artful things makes me happy.

I started my career in design but quickly shifted to development. I have been possessed with the journey of taking a design and transforming it into something to release and interact with other people.

I consider myself a methodical person and developer. I always liked the idea of following the path when you create things. Because of the consistency of the quality and reliability of your products. And I like to keep things simple and minimalistic. I am definitely not the brightest mind at work. I just believe that there is nothing you can't do when you really dedicate yourself to the making.

Outside of work; I like to run. I have run like 5-6 half marathons and numerous long-distance runs. And it keeps going. Besides that, I like to play video games and that helps me to blow off my steam. I like watching boring and sad movies. I like listening to old hard rock bands.
